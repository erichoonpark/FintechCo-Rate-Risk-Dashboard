# RISK-102 — Historical Stress Comparison

**Status:** Open  
**Priority:** Medium  
**Assignee:** Unassigned

## Summary
Overlay historical stress periods on the risk index chart for context.

## Description
Overlay the 2018 rate-hike cycle and the 2020 COVID shock on the existing risk index chart so stakeholders can benchmark current conditions against known stress periods.

## Acceptance Criteria
- 2018 rate-hike cycle period shaded or annotated on the chart
- 2020 COVID shock period shaded or annotated on the chart
- Overlays are visually distinct and labeled
- Does not clutter the existing risk index line
